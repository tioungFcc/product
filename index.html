specs

  • Reinforced privacy protection

    With MIUI 12, users will be able to get better control over permissions that are required for apps to function.

  • Design and animations

    Xiaomi has revamped the UI design for MIUI 12 and refreshed system-wide animations.

  • Multitasking

    MIUI 12 improves on multi-tasking by adding floating windows for apps that will help users easily move them, close or resize them with gestures.

  • Ultra Battery Saver

    MIUI 12 adds an ultra-battery saver mode that will restrict the more power-consuming features without affecting calls, messages, and network connectivity.

  • App Drawer

    MIUI usually follows the more iPhone-esque home screen with the absence of an app drawer. Now with MIUI 12, users will get an option to turn on the app drawer which will free up their home screen and show all the installed apps in one place.

  • Dark Mode

    MIUI 12 brings an enhanced Dark Mode that changes the colpur palette for system backgrounds, system apps, and third-party apps to a darker theme.
